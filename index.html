understory collective.

A shared digital forest. Plant a tree, leave a memory, and let your roots grow alongside strangers'.

A small tribute to belonging, and the quiet networks beneath our feet.

What you can do

  • Plant a tree (6 styles, 4 colors, 3 sizes) with a short memory.
  • Click someone else's tree to read theirs, and leave a 🌸 flower.
  • Press P to plant, Esc to cancel.

How it works

  • Real-time multiplayer via Firebase Firestore.
  • Trees sprout and grow over ~2 days; as new ones are planted, the oldest gently fade — so the forest never gets cluttered.
  • Scrub the “History of All Time” slider to rewind and watch the whole forest grow and fade.
  • Built with Three.js and WebGL.
  • Background music with mute.
