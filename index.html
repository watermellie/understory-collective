motherland.

A collaborative digital sanctuary. Motherland reimagines "home" as a shared space of belonging. Just as real forests share nutrients through underground networks, this ecosystem thrives on community. By putting down roots and leaving a memory, your tree grows alongside those of strangers, transforming isolated moments into a lush, collective human experience. The experience is also a gentle tribute to nature, inviting reflection on our ties to the earth, ecosystem fragility, and the vital importance of conservation. We don't just grow the forest; it grounds us in return.

Crafted with WebGL and Three.js, the frontend features a custom 3D globe with dynamic day/night lighting. The real-time, multi-user ecosystem is powered by Firebase Cloud Firestore, instantly broadcasting tree coordinates and user memories across all connected clients.
